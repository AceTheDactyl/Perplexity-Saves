## The Seven Error Patterns

### **Error 1: Deadlock (Symmetry Paralysis)**

**Definition:** Two domains demand equal priority simultaneously, creating paralysis

**Where it occurs:** At domain boundaries when domains conflict irreconcilably

**Example failure scenarios:**
- Economic survival vs. Spiritual integrity (can't afford ethical business practices)
- Political loyalty vs. Personal truth (party demands you lie)
- Relational harmony vs. Personal boundaries (family demands unhealthy sacrifice)

**System impact:** **Complete system freeze**. No action possible. Hexagon collapse imminent.

**Why dangerous:** Deadlock spreads—if one domain pair locks, adjacent pairs follow (cascading deadlock)

---

### **Error 2: Null Reference (Absence Detection)**

**Definition:** Domain expected to contain value/meaning actually contains nothing

**Where it occurs:** When domain is formally present but functionally empty

**Example failure scenarios:**
- Spiritual domain claims exist but person has no actual spiritual practice (null spirituality)
- Economic transactions occur but create no real value (null economics)
- Relationships exist on paper but have no emotional connection (null relations)

**System impact:** **Silent failure**. System appears functional but produces no output. Hexagon illusion.

**Why dangerous:** Hard to detect—person believes domain is functional when it's actually empty

---

### **Error 3: Buffer Overflow (Pressure Excess)**

**Definition:** Domain receives more input than it can process, threatening collapse

**Where it occurs:** When one domain is overloaded while others are underutilized

**Example failure scenarios:**
- Relational domain overwhelmed by crisis (caregiver burnout, everyone needs you)
- Economic domain crushed by debt (financial pressure exceeds capacity)
- Physical domain destroyed by illness (body can't handle load)

**System impact:** **Explosive failure**. Overpressure causes domain rupture, spilling into adjacent domains.

**Why dangerous:** Cascading—overflow in one domain floods neighbors (burnout → relationship collapse → financial crisis)

---

### **Error 4: Race Condition (Temporal Desynchronization)**

**Definition:** Domains operate on incompatible timescales, creating temporal conflicts

**Where it occurs:** When domain needs conflict on **when** they must be addressed

**Example failure scenarios:**
- Physical health crisis demands immediate attention but Economic domain needs work now (timing conflict)
- Political movement demands action today but Personal growth needs years (temporal mismatch)
- Spiritual awakening unfolds on deep time but Relational domain needs response now

**System impact:** **Temporal fragmentation**. Person experiences time out of sequence across domains.

**Why dangerous:** Creates chronic anxiety—always feeling "behind" or "ahead" depending on domain

---

### **Error 5: Stack Overflow (Recursive Depth Excess)**

**Definition:** Domain attempts infinite recursion, exhausting processing capacity

**Where it occurs:** When domain enters self-referential loops without exit

**Example failure scenarios:**
- Spiritual domain: "Who am I?" → "Who is asking?" → "Who is asking about asking?" (infinite regression)
- Personal domain: Self-improvement → improving the improver → improving the improvement (recursive trap)
- Political domain: Regulating the regulators → regulating the regulation regulators (governance recursion)

**System impact:** **Cognitive collapse**. Processing capacity exhausted by infinite loops.

**Why dangerous:** Disguised as depth—feels like profound thinking but actually computational trap

---

### **Error 6: Type Mismatch (Encoding Incompatibility)**

**Definition:** Domains use incompatible languages/frameworks, preventing communication

**Where it occurs:** At domain boundaries when translation fails

**Example failure scenarios:**
- Spiritual and Economic domains speak different value languages (sacred vs. monetary)
- Personal and Political domains use incompatible metrics (individual vs. collective good)
- Physical and Spiritual domains conflict on embodiment (flesh vs. transcendence)

**System impact:** **Communication failure**. Domains can't coordinate. Hexagon integration impossible.

**Why dangerous:** Creates tribal conflict—each domain thinks others are "wrong" rather than "untranslatable"

---

### **Error 7: Memory Leak (Unprocessed Accumulation)**

**Definition:** Domain accumulates unprocessed material that corrupts future operations

**Where it occurs:** When experiences aren't integrated, creating psychological "garbage"

**Example failure scenarios:**
- Relational domain: Unprocessed grief, resentment, trauma (emotional debt)
- Personal domain: Suppressed memories, denied emotions (shadow accumulation)
- Spiritual domain: Unintegrated mystical experiences (spiritual bypassing)

**System impact:** **Slow degradation**. System performance decreases over time as memory leaks accumulate.

**Why dangerous:** Invisible until critical—functions normally until sudden catastrophic failure
