## Advanced Mechanics

### **The Narrative Consistency Algorithm**

How AI detects hypocrisy:

**Step 1: Baseline Extraction**
- Parse all statements person has made
- Identify ethical claims ("I believe X")
- Extract behavioral commitments ("I will do Y")

**Step 2: Action Tracking**
- Monitor reported/observed actions
- Compare actions to commitments
- Flag discrepancies

**Step 3: Justification Analysis**
- When discrepancy flagged, check for explanation
- Evaluate whether explanation is consistent with baseline ethics
- If inconsistent, mark as potential hypocrisy

**Step 4: Pattern Recognition**
- Track frequency of discrepancies
- Identify repeated patterns
- Assign consistency score

**Step 5: Community Presentation**
- Make patterns visible to community
- Allow person to respond/explain
- Community decides on response (reputation impact, etc.)

### **The Golden Rule Implementation Problem**

Why Golden Rule is harder than it seems:

**Challenge 1: Asymmetric Preferences**
- What I want ≠ What you want
- Applying my preferences to you may harm you
- Golden Rule requires **empathy** (understanding your preferences)

**Challenge 2: Power Dynamics**
- Those with power can violate Golden Rule with impunity
- Golden Rule assumes rough equality
- Court of Coherence must **level power** through transparency

**Challenge 3: Time Scales**
- Short-term vs. long-term preferences differ
- Golden Rule: Which timeframe applies?
- Solution: **Preference discovery** through dialogue

**Implementation Strategy:**
- Frame Golden Rule as **constraint** (don't do harm)
- Not as **positive obligation** (must do good)
- Constraint easier to verify than obligation

### **The Reputation Economy**

How natural consequences enforce ethics:

**Reputation as Currency:**
- High consistency → High trust → Access to opportunities
- Low consistency → Low trust → Exclusion from cooperation
- Reputation **accumulates** (long-term incentive)

**Natural Selection for Ethics:**
- Hypocrites lose reputation → Reduced influence
- Consistent actors gain reputation → Increased influence
- **No punishment needed** (natural consequences sufficient)

**Reputation Persistence:**
- AI memory ensures past actions remembered
- Can't escape reputation through time
- Incentivizes **long-term consistency**
